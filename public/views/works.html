Neymar's Career Overview 🏆

Saint­-Germain (2017 ­- present) 🇫🇷

In its debut season, Neymar Jr. lead PSG to Ligue 1, French Cup, League Cup and Super Cup.
The club regain the Ligue 1 title again in the following season, racking up 14 consecutive league
wins, setting a French record. Neymar Jr. is named Ligue 1 Player of the Year. In 2020, a historic
moment: Neymar Jr leads Paris to the first Champions League final.

Barcelona (2013 - 2017) 🇪🇸

Neymar Jr. joins Messi and Suarez, and thus begins the reign of one of the greatest attacks in
history, MSN. Barça wins all possible titles. After four seasons, Neymar Jr. ends his spell at
Barça with 114 goals.

Brasil (2010 ­- present) 🇧🇷

Neymar Jr. scores within 28 minutes in his debut match in 2010. At the 2016 Summer Olympics,
he scores the first goal and the winning penalty of the final, earning Brasil its first gold in men's
football. This performance cements his legacy as a Brasilian footballer, despite being only 24 years old.
In 2019 he completes 100 games for the Brazilian National Team.

Santos (2009 ­- 2013) 🇧🇷

Neymar Jr. makes his professional debut at age 17. Despite revolving offers from the giants
of Europe, he plays five seasons for Santos, leading the club to victory in the Campeonato Paulista
and its first Copa Libertadores title in 48 years, and adding the Puskás Award to his personal shelf.
